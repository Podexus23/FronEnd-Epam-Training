The marathon is a long-distance foot race with a distance of 42.195 kilometres, usually run as a road race

The marathon can be completed by running or with a run/walk strategy. More than 800 marathons are held throughout the world each year, with the vast majority of competitors being recreational athletes, as larger marathons can have tens of thousands of participants.

The marathon was one of the original modern Olympic events in 1896 . The distance did not become standardized until 1921.

The distance is also included in the World Athletics Championships , which began in 1983.
